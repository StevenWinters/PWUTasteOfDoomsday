About Us.

Our website with the conjoined efforts of all 10-Curie students, aims to educate the people who would access our page. In this website you would all be able to learn more about the place we live in, the place in which contains much more information that a person might not know of. From the origins of the earth to keeping safe while living in it, by the end of this website, you will be taught lots of information!
